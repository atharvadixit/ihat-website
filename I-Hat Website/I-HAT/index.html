Frequently Asked Questions

  • What happens to the current switches in the house if I install this system ?

    They work as they have always done. Our system work along with your devices.

  • I move houses every few months. How portable is the system?

    It's quite easy to move from one home to another. All it takes is changing a few wire connections.

  • What happens if the internet network is down, Will the system work without internet?

    When you are at home, then no internet is required, as your phone and the home controller would be on the same wifi network. But when you are outside your home, then both the home controller and the phone will need internet access to talk to each other.

  • Does the system need any extra wiring?

    No, our system is sleek module making it to easily fit behind any switchboard and it works with the existing wiring inside the switchboard and switches.

  • Do I need to pay for installation of the system?

    No, you don't have to pay extra for installation.
